About The Program

The Women in Tech (WIT) program connects university students and recent graduates with experienced technology mentors. It enhances technical skills and prepares participants for STEM careers through mentorship, practical training, and networking opportunities. Courses include Cyber Security, SQL Fundamentals, Network Development, Project Management, and more. This program empowers participants to excel in the tech industry.

FAQs

Who can join the Women in Tech (WIT) program?

How can I apply for the program?

Apply via our website by filling out the form on the Apply Now page.

Is there a fee?

The program is free, providing equal opportunities for all.
